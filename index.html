PERSONAL INFO

ABOUT

I'm a web developer with three and a half years of expirience on building responsive, accessible, performant websites and interactive web applications as well as one year on WordPress websites. I have experience both in Front and Back End development, and I enjoy transforming a client's idea into an elegant, user-friendly website.

During my professional career as well as my university studies, working solo or as part of a team, I always target to write clean, easy to understand code and stay up to date with all tools and techniques.

EDUCATION

I am an Electrical and Computer Engineer and in 2017 I obtained my diploma from the corresponding engineering school of the Democritus University of Thrace with my major being in Electronic Engineering and Computer Science.

After my graduation, I attended a series of various seminars in order to expand my studies in the field of Web Development. Now I attend a graphic design seminar in order to improve my skills such as combine colors harmonically, highlight important information and improve user experience

EXPERIENCE

During my years in Media42 I had to either implement a graphics designer's idea or create from scratch a website (both Front and Back End). Furthermore, I was responsible for creating editor pages for clients, where they could import and change their content as well as creating and maintaining their databases.

On top of web design, I had to edit and create logos as well as process images and videos. For ensuring a qualitative outcome, I was participating in discussions and alignments with clients

MY SERVICES
FRONT END DEVELOPMENT
My approach focuses on creating responsive, accessible, functional, simple and user-friendly websites and web applications, using HTML, CSS and Javascript, for both users and business. I can design a website from scratch that match with the client or implement a graphics designer's idea with modern techniques.
BACK END DEVELOPMENT
Because of my studies I learned to think like a programmer so I always look for opportunities to solve real-world problems which led me to Back End development. Using PHP I feel that I can implement everything I envision for a project. I focus in writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code and and I'm constantly trying to expand my horizons through collaborations.
WORDPRESS DEVELOPMENT
In my opinion a WordPress developer need a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript in ordero to accurately portray the desired design, which I also have. I build sipmle or a complex WooCommerce websites on WordPress, using custom WordPress themes, plugins or forms from scratch, with a focus on performance, SEO and easy content management.
COOPERATION
Due to the fact that I have deep knowledge both in Front and Back End development, I can collaborate with Back End developers to integrate Front End code and vice versa. Moreover, attending a graphic design seminar helps me to unterstand and read graphic designer's draft as well as discuss it with them in order to transform it in code.
